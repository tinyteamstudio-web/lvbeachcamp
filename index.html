About LV Beach Camp

LV Beach Camp offers a perfect mix of native cottages, beachfront kubos, and glamping tents — all built for guests who want to unwind close to nature. Whether you’re traveling solo, with friends, or family, our beach camp welcomes you to relax, recharge, and reconnect with the sea.
